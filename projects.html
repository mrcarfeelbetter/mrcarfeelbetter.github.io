Our first project was a 1937 Packard. It was a challenging project, but also very fulfilling. Finding suitable replacement parts was quite the challenge, but we managed to source all of the parts from our various suppliers. The car’s exterior has been restored to its original shine. Seeing this car come back to life is a truly rewarding experience

Restoring a 1963 Mercedes-Benz 300SL Gullwing is a complex project that requires alot of attention to detail. This car’s unique features, such as its gull-wing doors, require precise alignment during the restoration process. Restoring a classic car like this is a labor of love, but the end result is a beautifully restored piece of automotive history.

The restoration of the 1956 Chevy Bel Air was an extremely rewarding project. It was mission of the heart that required a lot of hard work and attention to detail, but seeing the finished product made it all worth it. Finally, it is beautiful classic car that can be showcased and enjoyed for many years to come.
